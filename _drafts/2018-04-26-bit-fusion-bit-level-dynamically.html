Venue: ISCA 2018
Authors: Hardik Sharma, Jongse Park, ... , Hadi Esmaeilzadeh

Inspired by a previous called STRIPES, this paper proposes a 'reconfigurable architecture' to exploit varied bit-width requirements in neural networks to improve performance and energy efficiency. This accelerator supports both feed-forward (CNN, FC)  and recurrent (RNN, LSTM) layers.
